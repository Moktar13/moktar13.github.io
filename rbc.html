Royal Bank of Canada

RBC Mobile Banking application provides a user-friendly and feature-rich platform, offering seamless financial services, investment options, and account management for millions of clients.

I worked directly on RBC's mobile banking application - both during my time as a co-op student and during my time as a fulltime iOS developer.

During my time at RBC, I revamped the biometrics experience for a substantial user base, facilitated the migration of a large client group to RBC Mobile Banking, and enhanced account functionalities for widespread accessibility. My contributions also played a key role in enabling millions of users to seamlessly invest through the app, resulting in significant financial transactions within the initial launch period. Additionally, I contributed to the development of complex features in four major product releases.
