eSteth

Point-of-care patient assessment app.

eSteth is a mobile application that pairs with the eSteth device to provide real-time point-of-care patient assessment.

Over a 6-month period, I worked in partnership with Tech4Life Enterprises (the founder and creator of the eSteth device and application) on their mobile application. During this time I developed and implemented a COVID-19 self-assessment tool, a mock COVID-19 patient pre-screening form, a feature that allows users to save and share patient data, and improved the existing codebase and UI/UX.
